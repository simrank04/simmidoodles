About Me


Hey there! 👋 I’m Simran Kaur — a Toronto-based UI/UX & Front-End Designer, self-taught artist, and creative explorer. I love turning ideas into beautiful digital experiences (and sometimes into real-life art too!). 🎨

  • With 5+ years of experience, I design intuitive, user-friendly interfaces that don’t just look good—they actually get results. 💡
  • Creativity meets code in my world: Figma, Adobe Creative Suite, Canva, HTML, CSS, JavaScript, React.js, and Vue.js are my trusty sidekicks in bringing designs to life. 💻✨
  • From running Design Thinking workshops to optimizing websites for performance and SEO, I focus on creating seamless, engaging experiences that users love. 🚀
  • When I’m not designing or coding, you’ll find me painting, capturing sunsets with my camera, finding a dog to play with or finding inspiration in the little joys of life. 🌅🐶
