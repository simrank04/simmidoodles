UX Case Study

AAA Environmental

Professional cleanup and remediation services website designed to build trust and drive client engagement.

Role: UI/UX Designer, UX Researcher & Front-End Developer
Project Type: Static Responsive Website
Link: Visit AAA Environmental

Project Overview

🧹

The Service

AAA Environmental Services Inc. specializes in professional cleanup and remediation services. The project involved creating a comprehensive, modern website that effectively communicates the company's expertise and builds trust with potential clients.

⚠️

The Problem

The company lacked a strong online presence to communicate service expertise, establish credibility, and generate client inquiries. Without a professional website, they were missing opportunities to reach customers and differentiate from competitors in the remediation industry.

✨

The Solution

Designed and developed a clean, responsive website with intuitive navigation, compelling service descriptions, trust-building testimonials, and a professional brand identity. The site includes case studies showcasing past projects and a clear call-to-action for client inquiries.


Tools & Technologies

Design & Research

Figma, Adobe Illustrator, Procreate, Canva

Development

HTML5, CSS3, JavaScript, Bootstrap

Tools

Visual Studio Code, Git, Chrome DevTools
